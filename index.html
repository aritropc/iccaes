ABOUT ICCAES 2020

Welcome to the official website of IEM-IC 61, International Conference on Chemical and Environmental Sciences 2019 (ICCAES 2019). ICCAES 2019 will be held in Kolkata, India, during 19th-21st September, 2019. ICCAES 2019 aims to bring together innovative researchers, academicians and industrial experts under the common umbrella to encourage intellectual collaboration and networking.

The conference will address advancements currently witnessed in the fields of :

  • Spectroscopy and its Application
  • Synthetic Organic Chemistry
  • Environmental and Green Chemistry
  • Material Science and Nanotechnology
  • Polymer Chemistry
  • Synthetic Inorganic Chemistry and Bio Inorganic Chemistry
  • Energy Science and Technology
  • Medicinal and Bioorganic chemistry
  • Novel Approaches to Analytical and Bioanalytical Method
  • Food and Agro Chemistry
  • Computational Chemistry
  • Advancements in Biotechnology
  • Photocatalysis and Photoelectrocatalysis
  • Semiconductors and Sensors
  • Biochemistry and Structural Biology

The conference aspires to exhibit the innovative research and technical excellence of world-renowned scientists and academician both from pure and applied fields.

This conference aims to converge world-wide globally recognized research professionals. ICCAES 2019 includes plenary lectures, keynote address and invited talks besides oral and poster contribution in technical sessions. We hope and believe that our international conference ‘ICCAES 2019’ will be a great learning opportunity, enriching experience and overall exposure for all the participants.

English is the official language of the conference. We welcome paper submissions. Papers presented in the conference will be submitted for inclusion into Conference Abstract Booklet.

SUBMISSION

Abstract Submission Guidelines

  • Abstract should be prepared in Microsoft Word.
  • Please download the abstract template from the website.
  • The deadline for abstract submission is 5th September 2019. No abstracts will be accepted after this date.
  • Abstracts MUST be prepared in English.
  • Abstracts should not exceed 250 words.(Text only).
  • Maximum one figure and two references are allowed in the abstract.

Preparation of Abstracts

A one page abstract should be prepared following the instruction given in the abstract template.
